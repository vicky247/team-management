Welcome to Talent Management System!

The purpose of this project was to make projections for the amount of points a employee will score according to which best performers of the company can be identified and others can be nurtured. To accomplish this analysis, we first created data from to assemble a dataset of employee score stats from the years 2013 to 2016.

After assembling a clean dataset, we used dashboard to analyze and project employee performance stats. Factors we used to predict employee point totals included: Attendance, Projects, Task Reports, No.of Sales, Extra hours worked, Additional Job Roles Taken, Team Meetings attended, Experience and No.of Projects completed. This site provides the source data and visualizations created throughout the analysis.
